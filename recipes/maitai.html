Mai Tai

The Tiki Drink!

Probably the most iconic Tiki Drink, the Mai Tai can be found at just about every bar in the Tropics.

Ingredients

  • 1oz Dark Rum
  • 1oz Dark Jamaican Rum
  • .5oz Orange Curacao
  • 1oz Lime Juice
  • .5oz Orgeat
  • .25oz Simple Syrup

Steps

  1. Shake All Ingredients with Ice
  2. Strain into a Rocks Glass filled with ice
  3. Garnish with mint and lime
