Painkiller

The More You Drink, The Less it Hurts!

Created at the Soggy Dollar Bar on the Island of Jost Van Dyke in the British Virgin Islands.

Ingredients

  • 4oz Pineapple Juice
  • 1oz Orange Juice
  • 1oz Coco Lopez
  • 2.5oz Dark Jamaican Rum

Steps

  1. Shake ingredients with crushed ice
  2. Pour unstrained into glass or Tiki mug
  3. Dust with nutmeg and cinnamon
  4. Garnish with pineapple spear, cinnamon stick, and orange wheel
